What is Complaint Management?

Complaint management is the process of handling, managing, responding to, and reporting customer grievances. This process has a streamlined approach and constant monitoring to ensure faster resolutions.

How to lodge a complaint?

1. Create an account by registering or using google.

2. Go to the Grievance page to upload your complaint.

3. You can add images while filing your complaint.

4. After that you can able to see the complaint details in the dashboard.

5. You can see how much your complaint is processed in the dashboard.


What happens next..

➜The admin will go through the complaint that you have filed.

➜Then the admin will update whether they have accepted your complaint or not, you can see that in the 'User Status Page'.

➜If the admin thinks that they need more information about the complaint they will contact you by using the details that you have given.

➜The admin will work to deal with your complaint as soon as possible.

➜User can able to see how much the complaint have processed in the status page.
