What is Complaint Management?

Complaint management is the process of handling, managing, responding to, and reporting customer grievances.
This process has a streamlined approach and constant monitoring to ensure faster resolutions.

How to use

1. Create an account by registering or using google.
2. Go to the Grievance page to upload your complaint.
3. You can add images while filing your complaint.
4. After that you can able to see the complaint details in the dashboard.
5. You can see how much your complaint is processed in the dashboard.
